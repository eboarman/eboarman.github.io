Disney

Bekki often goes to Disney World with friends, especially Epcot and Hollywood Studios. One advantage of the Magic Band is that you are able to stay at Disney after the initial close, and continue to ride rollercoasters and walk around until a much later time.

Indianapolis

Another place Bekki has been to often is Indianapolis. As an American Legion employee, she goes for conventions and work events. In the spare time, she enjoys looking at the public art works, the zoo, and some of the beautiful architecture in the city.
