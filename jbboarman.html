A Historical Visit of York

Jan & Bill often travel to the UK to sightsee and visit family. Recently, they visited York, a city in Northeast England.It is the largest city in the district, and is a historical city that contains a lot of Roman influence. It's famous for its cathedrals and castle walls. It has beautifull preserved buildings from medieval times, Georgian town houses, Victorian railways, and many shopping centres. VisitYork.org
